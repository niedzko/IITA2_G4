Project Idea

Overview

My idea for a project is something I am tentatively calling the Present Planner (or The Forgetful Husband), for people like me that struggle with important occasions and making sure these occasions are memorable for my loved ones.

Motivation

Important dates like anniversaries, birthdays and Christmas should be a time of joy and celebration, but for me they a source of stress. Knowing these occasions need to be appropriately celebrated gives me a strong desire to get it right, but this is usually followed by anxiety and fear of failure. I’m sure I am one of many people out there who have disappointed their partner by failing to remember, or properly plan for, an anniversary!

Description

The service (or app) would have two sections, People and Events.

The people section will allow for input of people whose occasions you need to remember. The initial iteration would allow for basic data input on each person. This may include tracking history of previous gifts, like and dislikes, favourite places, sporting teams, etc. With the help of psychology and personality profiling professionals, future deployments may include personality trait questions that would suggest gifts ideas. Partnerships could then be formed with retail and experience providers as a means of monetising the service.

The Events section would, at first, be a simple calendar to enter dates of important events to remember and allow you to link people to dates. Christmas for example would be linked to multiple people. With the additional of personality profiling and partner providers, the service would eventually send alerts for experiences (such as concerts or sporting events) that someone in your tracking list would enjoy, or sales at their favourite retail brand outlets.

A premium paid service could potentially be added where the procurement, shipping and even card writing could be automated.

Tools and Technologies

This will require bringing together multiple technologies and expertise, such as CRM (customer relationship management) functionality, calendaring, webpage design, database integration and the building of intelligence and algorithms. B2B linkage, use of API’s and e-commerce would eventually be needed. All of this will need to be hosted on a cloud providers infrastructure such as AWS.

With the ultimate goal of providing an end-to-end procurement service, a very complicated service architecture will result.

Skills Required

Entrepreneurship will the be the primary requirement as this is essentially describing a start-up company. A team of developers with the relevant coding skills would start building this on cloud infrastructure with the aim of publishing API plug-ins for third-party providers. Project management and program management will keep it on track. Business Development people will work to bring partner providers on board.

Outcome

I have a friend who always remembers the special occasions and sends a card or gift without fail. I am always left think what a special and thoughtful friend that person is. I also acknowledge that it takes a certain personality with exceptional organisation skills to do that consistently.

This service, when is becomes a seamless experience, will put that superpower into the hands of us mere mortals!
